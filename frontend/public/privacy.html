Privacy Policy

App: TATETODO (todo.kaylee.jp)
Contact: kaylee@bekindlabs.com
Last updated: 2026-05-12

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your username (lowercase) and a bcrypt hash of your password. We do not store your plaintext password. We store the todo list content you create or add to.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to operate the TATETODO service — authenticating you, storing your lists, and syncing them across devices in real time.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. The service runs on a self-hosted server; no third-party analytics or tracking is used.

Data retention

Your data is retained while your account exists. To request deletion of your account and data, contact kaylee@bekindlabs.com.

AI integrations (MCP)

If you connect an AI assistant (such as Claude or ChatGPT) via the MCP server, that assistant will be able to read and write your todo lists according to the permissions you grant. The AI provider's own privacy policy governs how they handle any data sent to them.

Cookies and tokens

We use JWT bearer tokens (stored client-side) for authentication. No tracking cookies are set.
